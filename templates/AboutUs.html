About This Platform

This website is specially developed for Primary 1 students from Sekolah Kebangsaan (SK) who are enrolled in our learning centre. Teachers can upload video lessons or notes and create exercises for students, while students can view these resources to support their learning.


Access and Registration

Both teachers and students are required to register and log in to access the main content. However, the Homepage, About Us, and Topic Overview pages are available to everyone before registration.


What Do We Offer?

The learning center offers Chinese, Malay, English, Mathematics, and Science for SJK (C) and SK students, while SMK students are provided with Malay, English, Mathematics, Science, and History.
